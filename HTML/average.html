Calculate the Average

Read me first

Enter a series of numbers and then see the average of your numbers. To do so, type your number in the box and click Enter to enter it. Follow the prompts above the box. Please note the following rules.

Please note the following rules.

  • You must enter at least 6 numbers.
  • Each number should have at least 3 digits.
  • Your numbers should not contain any zeros.

When you are done, click the stop button and you will see your average displayed. You will also be able to display the numbers you entered.
